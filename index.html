About ZAHRA

Moroccan heritage, crafted with intention

Our Jelabas celebrate the art of Moroccan tailoring: the patience of artisans, the rhythm of ateliers, and the elegance of tradition.

A story stitched into every silhouette

The Jelaba is more than clothing—it is presence. It carries the elegance of Moroccan culture, shaped through generations of knowledge.

We focus on refined drape, harmonious color palettes, and subtle gold details that echo traditional motifs—reinterpreted for modern elegance.
